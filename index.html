About Me

My Profile

Hi, I’m Daniel. I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Biostatistics at the University of Abomey-Calavi, building on my background in Mathematics and Statistics. I have strong expertise in data analysis and interpretation, with proficiency in R, MATLAB, Stata, and SPSS.

I’m driven by a passion for using data to solve real-world problems, especially in research and public health. My training in Biostatistics has honed my ability to turn complex datasets into meaningful, actionable insights. I’m always open to conversations feel free to reach out to discuss about data analysis and collaboration opportunities.

Career Vision

My goal is to harness cutting-edge AI and advanced data analysis techniques to drive innovation across research and business applications. With a strong foundation in Biostatistics, I aim to design robust studies, analyze and interpret complex data, develop predictive models, and provide actionable insights to support evidence-based decisions in healthcare, research, and industry.

In addition, I have expanded my expertise through a short course in Marketing at the University Rey Juan Carlos (Madrid, Spain), allowing me to integrate data-driven strategies into business and research contexts effectively. I am passionate about leveraging data to transform industries, optimize decision-making, and create meaningful impact.

Personal Philosophy

Think about this: I'm a Biostatistician by profession, which means I live in the world of numbers, patterns, and evidence. My day job is to untangle complex health data like the results of a clinical trial or patterns in disease spread and translate it into clear, meaningful stories that can save lives and shape better health policies. I see the hidden narratives hidden inside spreadsheets and databases.

But my curiosity doesn't switch off when I leave my work desk.

My deeper, personal passion is understanding a different kind of complex system: human beings.

I am fascinated by what makes people tick, like why we communicate the way we do, why some conversations turn needlessly rude, and how we can choose empathy over anger. I believe that the same skills I use as a statistician to do observation, pattern recognition, and seeking truth apply to understanding human interactions.

You'll often find me:

  • Thinking deeply about why "hurt people hurt people."
  • Valuing quiet, meaningful conversations over loud, superficial debates.
  • Choosing silence not out of fear, but as a conscious strategy to avoid negativity and maintain my peace.

In a world that often mistakes loudness for strength, I find power in calmness. I am drawn to the idea of quiet leadership: influencing others not through authority, but through patience, deep listening, and emotional intelligence.

So, whether I'm analyzing data to improve public health or reflecting on how to navigate a difficult conversation, my goal is the same: to bring clarity, understanding, and a positive impact one number, and one interaction, at a time.

Generative AI Course

The Generative AI for Work & Research course is an 8-week live cohort program offered by GRAPH Courses, University of Geneva. It focuses on leveraging no-code AI tools for task automation, research, and communication.

1

Websites, Documents & Presentations

Learn how large language models (LLMs) work. Generate text for papers or grants, create project websites with no-code tools, format documents efficiently, and design clear presentations or posters using AI assistants.

Topics
  • How LLMs are trained and an overview of popular LLM providers
  • Generating Markdown, LateX and HTML outputs
  • Building simple research websites with ChatGPT Canvas and Replit
  • Using Markdown for structured research documents
  • Introduction to AI presentation tools for scientific communication
2

AI-Automated Data Analysis

Master AI tools for analyzing data, manipulating files, and creating automated reports. Use Julius, ChatGPT Code Interpreter and Windsurf to process datasets, visualize results, and generate reports quickly.

Topics
  • Use ChatGPT Code Interpreter for quick data analysis
  • Build automated dashboards with TheBricks
  • Process and transform datasets with Julius
  • Implement batch file conversion and manipulation
  • Create automated reports and data visualizations
  • Design spreadsheet automations for recurring analyses
3

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Learn AI techniques for literature search, text analysis, and knowledge synthesis. Explore how AI can accelerate information gathering, identify patterns in text data, and organize complex information.

Topics
  • Conducting efficient literature reviews with Elicit and LiteRev
  • Implementing text classification for document organization
  • Applying sentiment analysis to identify trends and opinions
  • Creating knowledge maps from document collections
  • Designing automated summarization workflows
  • Building information synthesis systems for research topics
4

Custom GPTs and Chatbots

Dive deeper into research assistant creation by leveraging advanced LLM architecture. Build chatbots for research assistance and knowledge management.

Topics
  • LLM chat architecture, context windows, and RAG systems
  • Building custom GPTs for specific use cases
  • Creating interactive chatbots with Botpress
  • Integrating knowledge bases with your research materials
5

AI for Task Automation

Discover practical strategies for automating repetitive research tasks through AI-driven flows. Set up email response automations, design AI agents for literature monitoring, and orchestrate complex workflows.

Topics
  • Introduction to AI automation and agent-based systems
  • Setting up email response automations
  • Building AI agents for complex automation flows
  • Browser control agents for web-based tasks
6

GenAI for Communication and Outreach

Explore AI-driven approaches for research dissemination, focusing on creating assets for sharing your findings. Learn to develop methods for summarizing research and design visuals for publications.

Topics
  • AI-generated research summaries for different audiences
  • Designing scientific figures and diagrams with AI tools
  • AI-generated video abstracts and explanations
  • Prompt engineering for scientific visualization
  • Content scheduling for research communication
7

Capstone Project & Showcase

Collaborate with peers and instructors to create and refine a final AI solution, tool or presentation. Receive real-time feedback and troubleshoot technical challenges.

Topics
  • Project progress check
  • Peer feedback sessions
  • Final project presentations
  • Public Q&A session

About GRAPH Courses

At GRAPH Courses, they're committed to making high quality data science and AI education accessible and impactful. Based at the University of Geneva, Switzerland, their programs combine academic rigor with practical, industry relevant skills.

Their bootcamp style approach ensures an immersive learning experience. You'll gain technical know-how, build real world AI projects, and in fact set yourself apart in the job market.

What sets them apart is their supportive learning ecosystem. With live workshops, personalized project reviews, and a global community of peers, you're never alone. Their experienced instructors and mentors are dedicated to your success.

As a non-profit backed by academic institutions, they offer this comprehensive bootcamp at an affordable price without compromising on quality-Speaking from experience.
